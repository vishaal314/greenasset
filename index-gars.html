About Us

Local E-Waste Experts in Greater Noida

Green Asset Recycling Solutions (GARS) is a local e-waste aggregation and collection business serving the Greater Noida and Noida region. We bridge the gap between households, offices, and schools that want to dispose electronics responsibly and the authorized recycling ecosystem.

We work exclusively with licensed and authorized recyclers, ensuring that every device we collect is processed in full compliance with environmental regulations — keeping hazardous materials out of landfills and our communities.

Whether you are a mobile repair shop with old handsets, an IT company upgrading infrastructure, or a household with a pile of old gadgets, we make disposal effortless and free.

  • Free pickup for households, offices, schools, and repair shops
  • Serving Greater Noida, Noida, and Noida Extension
  • All material routed to authorized, licensed recyclers only
  • Bulk pickup support for IT companies and data centres
  • Quick 24-hour response and flexible scheduling
  • Helping businesses meet CSR and green disposal goals
